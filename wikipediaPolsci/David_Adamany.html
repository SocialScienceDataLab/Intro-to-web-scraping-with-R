David Adamany

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

David Adamany (born September 23, 1936)[1] was Temple University's eighth president,[2] and the 8th president of Wayne State University. One of Wayne State University's libraries, David Adamany Undergraduate Library, was his creation.

Adamany later served for a time as head of the Detroit Public School District.

Education[edit]

A.B., J.D., Harvard University; M.A., Ph.D., University of Wisconsin-Madison
