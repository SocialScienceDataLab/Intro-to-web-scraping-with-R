Timothy E. Cook (1954–2006) was an American scholar of mass communications, Professor of Journalism at Louisiana State University. He is known for his books on the interaction of politics and the media, and also as an influence on journalism research and education.[1] He was best known [2] for his widely-reviewed book "Governing with the News.
